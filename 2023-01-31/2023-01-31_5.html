Morgan Wallen's 'Everything I Love' Sticks With a Winning Formula

Summary: After announcing his colossal, 36-track One Thing at a Time album on Monday (Jan. 3), Morgan Wallen wasted no time in introducing fans to some of the project's tracks. He dropped three new songs — "Everything I Love," "Last Night" and "I Wrote the Book" — at midnight, and taken all together, the songs represent a mix of familiar subject matter and uncharted ground. "Everything I Love," perhaps the most radio-ready and traditionally country of the trio, doesn't deviate too far from what Wallen already knows will work. It's a heartbreak ballad with honky-tonk leanings, and plenty of references to trucks, whiskey and back roads. The singer is likely to show more range as more songs emerge — 36 tracks is a lot, after all.
