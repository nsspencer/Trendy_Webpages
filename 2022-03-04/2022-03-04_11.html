Chris Olave, Garrett Wilson run 40-yard dash in under 4.4 seconds at NFL combine

Former Ohio State wide receivers Chris Olave and Garrett Wilson both clocked 40-yard dash times under 4.4 seconds at the NFL scouting combine in Indianapolis. The official times were 4.38 seconds for Wilson and 4.39 seconds for Olave, making them the seventh and eighth-fastest receivers who ran on Thursday night. ESPN reported they’re only the second pair of teammates since 2006 to post sub-4.4 second times during the same combine. In 2019, Parris Campbell ran the 40-yard dash in 4.31 seconds, and Terry McLaurin finished in 4.35 seconds. Baylor wide receiver Tyquan Thornton had the fastest 40-yard dash time this week at 4.28 seconds.

It took less than 4.4 seconds for Chris Olave and Garrett Wilson to solidify their status as first-round draft picks at the NFL Scouting Combine.
