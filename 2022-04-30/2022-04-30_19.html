A dual-threat quarterback and linebacker in high school, leading his team to three state championships, Rodriguez became a three-year starter at linebacker and two-year team captain at Oklahoma State, finishing fifth in school history with 409 career tackles. He reached triple-digit tackles in 2019 and 2021, and also notched 16.5 tackles for loss this past season with four forced fumbles and five passes defended. A little bit undersized at 5-foot-11, 232 pounds, Rodriguez makes up for it with terrific speed (4.52 in the 40) and instincts. He played weakside linebacker for the Cowboys and was a core special teamer, which is where he could make his biggest impact early in his NFL career.
