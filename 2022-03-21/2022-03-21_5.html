Jergens Ultra Healing Moisturizer Recall Over Harmful Bacteria in Lotion

Summary: Selected bottles of one of Jergens' moisturizers are being removed from the market by its manufacturing company, which identified the threat of a possible bacteria within the product. According to Kao USA Inc., the presence of the bacteria Pluralibacter gergovia was discovered in some lots of its 3- and 10-ounce Ultra Healing moisturizer. The company has urged customers to check that their bottles of Ultra Healing moisturizer aren't part of the lots at risk. Pluralibacter gergovia is part of a novel species of bacteria first discovered in the 1980s.
