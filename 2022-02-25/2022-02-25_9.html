Bill Paxton’s Family Reaches $1M Settlement With Anesthesiologist Group Over Actor’s Death

An anesthesiologist medical group has settled for $1 million its part in a wrongful death lawsuit brought by Bill Paxton’s family over the actor’s death in 2017 following an allegedly botched heart surgery. General Anesthesia Specialists Partnership maintained that it bears no responsibility in Paxton’s death. The claims against General Anesthesia Specialists Partnership included wrongful death, survival action and breach of fiduciary duty. An L.A. judge ruled in January that Paxton’s family can seek punitive damages for claims that the hospital engaged in a cover-up to conceal its negligence. Attorneys for General Anesthesia Specialists Partnership and Paxton’s family didn’t immediately respond to requests for comment.
