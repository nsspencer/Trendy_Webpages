Chelsea vs Lille live score and result updates: Champions League latest as Havertz and Pulisic goals secure Chelsea win

Chelsea took a strong hold of their Champions League Round of 16 tie against Lille thanks to a 2-0 victory at Stamford Bridge. Thomas Tuchel opted to deploy Kai Havertz as a striker over Romelu Lukaku, and the decision had an instant impact with Havertz heading home the opening goal after just eight minutes. Lille had spells of possession in the opening half but were unable to test Edouard Mendy in the Chelsea goal. The Blues then took control of the tie in the 63rd minute as Christian Pulisic finished past Leo Jardim after a fantastic run and pass from N'Golo Kante.
