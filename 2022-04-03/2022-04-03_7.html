Unstoppable Abraham close to breaking English Serie A record

Summary: Tammy Abraham is continuing to shine with Roma and he’s close to breaking the record for the most goals scored by an Englishman in a single Serie A season. The 24-year-old English striker joined the Giallorossi in a deal worth €40m from Chelsea last summer, taking the risk with a move abroad instead of joining the interested Arsenal. This bravery has paid off and he’s now a star in the Italian capital, being incredibly decisive under Jose Mourinho. As highlighted by Opta, Abraham has scored 15 goals in 29 Serie A appearances this season and he’s just one away from matching the record of most goals scored by an Englishman in a single Serie A season, set by Gerald Hitchens with Inter in the 1961-62 season. Only two players have ever scored more goals than Abraham in their debut Roma season – Rodolfo Volk, who netted 21 times in the 1929-30 season, and Pedro Manfredini, who scored 16 goals in the 1959-60 campaign.
