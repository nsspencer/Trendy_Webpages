As Shaheen Holloway beats Kentucky, Seton Hall AD who hired him is 'beyond thrilled'

Summary: SAN DIEGO — Shaheen Holloway and his Saint Peter’s basketball team may have shocked the college basketball world by toppling second-seeded Kentucky in the NCAA Tournament’s first round Thursday. He guided the Peacocks to their first NCAA Tournament berth since 2011 and now their first Big Dance victory ever — and with an upset of one of the sport's behemoths.
