Coachella 2022: How parking at the festival benefits a local school district

Summary: If you’re parking at the 2022 Coachella music festival, chances are you might be leaving your car on property owned by a local school district. Parking is free for Coachella 2022 attendees, but Goldenvoice, the promoter for Coachella and Stagecoach, pays the Coachella Valley Unified School District approximately $200,000 annually to lease 62 acres of land near the festival grounds in Indio to use as parking during its big events. In addition, Goldenvoice makes an annual donation of $385,000 to the school district to support student programs. The promoter and the school district entered into a contract in 2018. Goldenvoice’s music festivals did not take place in 2020 or 2021 due to the COVID-19 pandemic, so the promoter did not make payments or donations to the school district those years.
