Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore, Sonic 2 on Rakuten TV

Summary: Premium VoD platform Rakuten TV has announced the release of their latest blockbuster titles for June. In SVoD, new period drama Becoming Elizabeth will be heading to Rakuten TV from June 12th.
