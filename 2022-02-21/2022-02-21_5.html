Is the Post Office Open on Presidents Day? USPS, UPS, FedEx Hours and Delivery Information

Presidents Day is a federal holiday observed on the third Monday of every February, which this year falls on February 21. Also known as Washington's Birthday, Presidents Day was initially designed to celebrate the birth of the country's first president on February 22, 1732. UPSAll UPS services remain open on Presidents Day. FedExMost FedEx services are operating on normal hours on Presidents Day, except for FedEx Ground Economy and FedEx Express, which are offering a modified service, according to the company's website. For FedEx Ground Economy, "deliveries may be delayed" because of the USPS observation of Presidents Day, the company said.
