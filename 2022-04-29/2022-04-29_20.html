They also noted that Manatee County has been named a Tree City USA community fort the 13th straight year.The median beautification program is being rolled out in various locations around the county. Residents can expect to see more of the beautification projects coming soon… to a median near them. brief video of the Arbor Day 2022 ceremony and comments from those on hand can be found on the County YouTube Channel.
