ACME Lithium closes US$3M funding deal with Lithium Royalty and Waratah Capital

Summary: ACME said it received US$650,000 from the grant of project royalties on its Manitoba lithium assets and US$2.35 million by way of the non-brokered private placement. It added that Lithium Royalty Corp purchased the 2% gross overriding royalty on lithium or other metals from ACME's Euclid-Cat Lake and Shatford Lake projects, the proceeds of which will be directed to exploration on those properties. The private placement consisted of 3,179,500 ACME Lithium units, issued at a price of C$0.94 per unit, with each unit comprised of one common share plus one-half of one common share purchase warrant. As a result of the funding, ACME said its working capital is about C$7.2 million with no debt. Led by an experienced team, ACME Lithium is focused on acquiring, exploring and developing battery metal projects in partnership with leading technology and commodity companies.
