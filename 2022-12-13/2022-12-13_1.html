A Reuters poll of 13 analysts indicated that the consumer price index jumped 5.9% last month with food costs tamed slightly by price freezes. "The deceleration was mainly explained by a lower increase in food prices," said Lautaro Moschet, an economist at Libertad y Progreso, pointing to the impact of food price freezes.
