Guinness names Shanghai eatery as world's highest restaurant in a building

In February, officials from the Guinness World Records awarded the accolade to the Heavenly Jin restaurant inside the J Hotel Shanghai Tower in Shanghai, China. J Hotel Shanghai TowerAs one would expect from the world's highest restaurant in a building, Heavenly Jin offers incredible views of the Shanghai skyline. Sitting at a height of 441.3 meters, it's located on Level 122 of the world's highest building -- Burj Khalifa, and opened in 2011. General manager of the J Hotel Shanghai Tower, Jenny Zhang (left) receives the Guinness World Record certificate for highest restaurant in a building. HECTOR RETAMAL/AFP/Getty ImagesAs for the J Hotel Shanghai Tower, it opened in mid-2021 and claims to be the world's highest hotel in a building.
